Created: Circa 12 July 2021 Last Modified: 12 October 2021 Commit Title: Create Pizza Delivery Paraplegic Demo.gif

Pizza Delivery Paraplegic is a 2D platformer in which the player plays as a pizza delivery man who uses a grappling hook to make their deliveries. The game is suited for individuals looking for a quick, mechanically intensive platformer.

Programs Used
  • Art: Pyxel Edit
  • Sound: SFXR and Audacity(sound clipping)
  • Godot 3 Engine
Lessons Learned:
  • Playtesting is key to making a product feel natural
  • Art is not in my skillset.
  • Sounds effects do not have to be anything special, as long as it fits with the event
